Venue

501 Union / 501 Union St, Brooklyn, NY

Located in Gowanus, Brooklyn, the 501 Union building dates back to 1916 and once served as a classic car restoration shop. It was transformed into a private events venue in 2013 and now provides a unique backdrop for stylish weddings, corporate events, and photo shoots.

For traveling to the ceremony, we recommend one of the following:

• Uber/Lyft/Taxi

• Take the F or G train to the Bergen. Walk 5 blocks.

• Take the R stop to Union St. Walk 3 blocks.

Pro Tip: If taking the subway, please allow extra time for weekend schedules ☺
